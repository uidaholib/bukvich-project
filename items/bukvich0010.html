Historical Background:
Written for Al Gimberling, the piece is based off his initials "A & G." The spelling of the 4th movement is a hymn but was referring to him, Al Gimberling. During the first rehearsal of this piece, Al was recorded to have broken into tears from the content of the 4th movement.
